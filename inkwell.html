Rules at a glance

  • One secret word, five letters, six guesses.
  • Every guess must be a valid English word.
  • Letters can repeat — read colours carefully when they do.
  • Use the on-screen keys or your physical keyboard. Backspace deletes, Enter submits.
  • Your wins, streak, and guess distribution are saved on this device.
